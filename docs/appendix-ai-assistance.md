> "I am preparing a technical assessment for a Technical Lead position at Sura. Help me develop these deliverables."

## Refinement Process

From this starting point, the architecture, framework design, diagrams, and decision records were iteratively refined through a series of follow-up exchanges focused on specific technical decisions, MuleSoft API-led connectivity patterns, resilience patterns (retry, circuit breaker, bulkhead, idempotency), and observability design.

All technical decisions, design choices, and written content reflect the candidate's own knowledge and judgment.
